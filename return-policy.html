Return & Exchange Policy

Effective Date: November 11, 2025

Our Commitment

At Sakr Store, customer satisfaction is our priority. We want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If for any reason you're not satisfied, we offer a fair and transparent return and exchange policy.

Return Policy (7 Days)

Eligibility for Returns

You may return products within 7 days of receiving your order if the following conditions are met:

  • The item must be in its original, new, and unused condition
  • Products that came factory-sealed must remain sealed and unopened
  • All original packaging, accessories, manuals, and documentation must be included
  • The product must not show any signs of use, wear, or damage
How to Initiate a Return:
Contact us via WhatsApp at 01108563748 with your order details and reason for return. Our team will guide you through the process.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item:

  • Processing time and method will be confirmed via WhatsApp
  • Refunds will be issued through the same payment method used for purchase
  • No restocking fees apply for eligible returns

Exchange Policy (15 Days)

Eligibility for Exchanges

We offer a 15-day exchange policy for products that meet the following criteria:

  • The item must be in its original, new, and unused condition
  • Factory-sealed products must remain sealed and unopened
  • All original packaging and accessories must be intact
  • Exchange must be for a product of equal or greater value
How to Request an Exchange:
Contact us via WhatsApp at 01108563748 to arrange an exchange. We'll help you select a suitable replacement product.

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Products with broken seals or signs of use
  • Items damaged due to misuse or negligence
  • Products missing original packaging or accessories
  • Customized or personalized items (if applicable)

Defective or Damaged Products

If you receive a defective or damaged product:

  • Contact us immediately via WhatsApp at 01108563748
  • Provide photos/videos showing the defect or damage
  • We will arrange for a replacement or full refund at no additional cost
  • All manufacturing defects are covered by our 30-day store warranty
Important: Please inspect your order upon delivery and report any issues within 24 hours for the fastest resolution.

Warranty Information

All products from Sakr Store come with:

  • 30-day store warranty against manufacturing defects
  • Official manufacturer warranty (where applicable)

For warranty claims, contact us via WhatsApp with your order details and a description of the issue.

Important Notes

  • Return and exchange requests must be initiated within the specified timeframes
  • Products must be returned in their original condition to qualify
  • Shipping costs for returns/exchanges will be discussed and confirmed via WhatsApp
  • We reserve the right to refuse returns/exchanges that don't meet our policy criteria
