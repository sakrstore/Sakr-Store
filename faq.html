Orders & Ordering

How do I place an order?

Placing an order is simple:

  • Browse our products and add items to your cart
  • Click the cart icon and review your items
  • Fill in your delivery details (name, address, phone)
  • Click "Complete Order via WhatsApp"
  • Confirm your order through WhatsApp with our team

Our team will confirm availability, shipping costs, and delivery time via WhatsApp.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Yes! Contact us immediately via WhatsApp at 01108563748 if you need to modify or cancel your order. If your order hasn't been shipped yet, we can make changes or process a cancellation.

How can I track my order?

We provide order updates via WhatsApp throughout the delivery process. You'll receive notifications when your order is confirmed, shipped, and out for delivery. For real-time updates, contact us on WhatsApp.

Do you have a minimum order amount?

No, there is no minimum order amount! You can order as few or as many items as you need. Shipping costs will be confirmed via WhatsApp based on your location and order details.

Shipping & Delivery

Where do you ship?

We ship nationwide across Egypt! No matter where you are, we can deliver to your location. We're based in El Shorouk and Zagazig but serve customers all over the country.

How long does delivery take?

Estimated delivery time is 2-7 business days depending on your location. Deliveries to major cities are typically faster, while more remote areas may take the full 7 days. We'll provide a more specific estimate when you place your order.

How much is shipping?

Shipping costs vary based on your location and order size. The exact shipping fee will be confirmed via WhatsApp when you place your order. This ensures transparent pricing with no surprises.

Do you offer express or same-day delivery?

Currently, we don't offer express or same-day delivery. However, we work hard to process and ship orders quickly. Standard delivery takes 2-7 business days nationwide.

Payment Methods

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple convenient payment methods:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD) - Pay when you receive your order
  • Mobile Wallets - Vodafone Cash, Orange Money, etc.
  • InstaPay - Quick bank transfers

Payment details will be confirmed via WhatsApp after placing your order.

Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?

Yes! Cash on Delivery is available for all orders across Egypt. Simply pay when your order arrives. This is our most popular payment option.

Are my payment details secure?

Absolutely! We prioritize your security. If paying via mobile wallet or InstaPay, transactions are processed through secure, trusted platforms. For Cash on Delivery, you pay the delivery person directly—no sensitive information is shared online.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy. Items must be in original, unused condition with factory seals intact (if applicable). All original packaging and accessories must be included.

To initiate a return, contact us via WhatsApp at 01108563748 with your order details.

We're sorry if that happens! Contact us immediately via WhatsApp at 01108563748 with photos/videos of the defect. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost.

How long does a refund take?

Refund processing time and method will be confirmed via WhatsApp after we receive and inspect your returned item. We aim to process refunds as quickly as possible.
